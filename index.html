Sheep Farm

The Farm Sheep project is dedicated to sustainable and efficient sheep farming, focusing on high-quality wool, meat, and dairy production. By integrating modern farming techniques with traditional practices, the initiative aims to promote eco-friendly, humane, and profitable sheep farming.

Benefits of Raising Sheep

1

Meat Production Provides high-quality lamb or mutton for consumption or sale.

2

Milk Production Sheep's milk is used for cheese and dairy products.

3

Wool Production Source of wool for textiles and clothing.

4

Low Maintenance Requires less care compared to other livestock.

5

Land Management Controls weeds and improves pasture quality through grazing.

6

Manure Natural fertilizer for farming.
